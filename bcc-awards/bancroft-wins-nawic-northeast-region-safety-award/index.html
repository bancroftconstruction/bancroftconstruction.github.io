Bancroft wins NAWIC Northeast Region safety award

Bancroft was recently named the recipient of the National Association of Women in Construction’s 2017 Northeast Region Safety Excellence Award! Bancroft topped 15 others from Washington, D.C. to Maine for the award. Bancroft’s win at the chapter and regional level qualifies us for the national award, which will be presented at NAWIC’s 62nd Annual Meeting & Education Conference in Anaheim, California this August.
