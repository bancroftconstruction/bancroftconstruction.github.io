DCA CONSTRUCTION EXCELLENCE

For over 20 years, the State of Delaware’s Office of Management and Budget has presented these prestigious awards in partnership with the Delaware Contractors Association. The awards recognize those projects that bring distinction, skill, quality and integrity to the construction industry in Delaware.

ABC EXCELLENCE IN CONSTRUCTION

The Delaware Chapter of the Associated Builders and Contractors Excellence in Construction awards are an opportunity to have your project(s) join other Merit Shop projects and be recognized by ABC as the “Best of the Best” in the construction industry. This competition is intended to create a greater awareness in the State of Delaware of the quantity and quality of work being produced by members of ABC Delaware and to continue to raise the standards throughout the construction industry.

SAFETY AWARDS

Bancroft has been fortunate to win a variety of safety awards at both the local and National level. Bancroft is a multiyear recipient of the Governor’s Safety Award, which was created 15 years ago and is co-sponsored by DCA and the Delaware Department of Labor. The award recognizes Delaware businesses for their safety performance as well as the emphasis that they place on safety in the workplace.
