Guiding Principles

Act with Integrity

Have an unwavering commitment to do what’s right regardless of the circumstances and uphold the highest ethical standards

Have Concern for Others

Possess a genuine compassion for the well-being of others

Establish Accountability

Accept personal responsibility for our actions and decisions and their impact on each other and our stakeholders

Be Fair

Demonstrate honesty and fairness in all matters and create win/win solutions

Lighten Up!

Seize opportunities to celebrate our stakeholders as they are vital to our success

Demonstrate Agility

Commit to constant innovation, nimbleness and flexibility when delivering our services and solving challenges

Encourage Collaboration

Promote a spirit of cooperation by communicating and working as a team with internal and
external stakeholders

Be Trustworthy

Commit to Continuous Improvement

Fulfill our promises and obligations and openly communicate if there are changes to circumstances
